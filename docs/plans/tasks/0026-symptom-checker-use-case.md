# Task 0026: Symptom Checker Use Case

## Problem
Users searching health symptoms online often get overwhelmed by worst-case-scenario results. Booey can provide a friendly, guided experience that helps people understand their symptoms and decide on next steps.

## Solution
Add a new "What's Wrong With Me?" use case under Health & Wellness that walks users through describing their symptoms with engaging question types (tagCloud, spectrum, imageSelect, emojiScale) and produces helpful, non-diagnostic health guidance.
