# Booey UX Audit — Head of UI/UX Perspective
**Date:** February 14, 2026  
**Target Audience:** 40-60 year olds (non-tech-savvy)  
**Current State:** Production MVP at booey.ai

---

## Executive Summary

Booey's core functionality works well, but the UX feels like an MVP. For our 40-60 year old demographic, we need to reduce cognitive load, increase trust signals, and make every interaction feel warm and approachable (not clinical).

**Priority Areas for Phase 6:**
1. Visual design & illustrations
2. Trust & credibility signals  
3. Onboarding & education
4. Wizard experience enhancements
5. Results page improvements
6. Accessibility & age-friendly design

---

## Landing Page Issues

### Visual Design
- **Issue:** Plain white backgrounds, no visual hierarchy beyond text
- **Impact:** Feels sterile, doesn't engage emotional connection
- **For 40-60s:** Need warmth, approachability, human touch

### Use Case Discovery
- **Issue:** 19 use cases in long scroll, no search, no grouping beyond basic filters
- **Impact:** Overwhelming, hard to scan, "paradox of choice"
- **For 40-60s:** Need curated recommendations, clear popular choices

### Trust Signals
- **Issue:** No testimonials, user count, reviews, or "as seen in" badges
- **Impact:** Hesitation to try ("Is this legit? Who else uses this?")
- **For 40-60s:** Need explicit social proof, security indicators

### Call-to-Action
- **Issue:** "Try it →" is just text, not a clear button
- **Impact:** Unclear what happens next, weak conversion signal
- **For 40-60s:** Need obvious, large, friendly CTAs

---

## Wizard Experience Issues

### Progress & Orientation
- **Issue:** Progress bar exists but no ETA, no "what's next" preview
- **Impact:** Uncertainty about time commitment
- **For 40-60s:** Need clear expectations ("Just 3 quick questions, takes 30 seconds")

### Form Clarity
- **Issue:** Plain textareas/dropdowns, no inline examples or help text
- **Impact:** Anxiety about "doing it wrong"
- **For 40-60s:** Need abundant examples, reassurance, "no wrong answers"

### Loading States
- **Issue:** Abrupt transition between questions and to results
- **Impact:** Feels janky, no feedback loop
- **For 40-60s:** Need smooth transitions, "AI is thinking..." states

---

## Results Page Issues

### Presentation
- **Issue:** Great content but plain text layout, no visual interest
- **Impact:** Feels like a wall of text, hard to scan
- **For 40-60s:** Need structured visuals, clear sections, printability

### Actionability
- **Issue:** No save/share/print/copy buttons, no iteration options
- **Impact:** User has to manually copy-paste, can't easily share
- **For 40-60s:** Need one-click actions ("Email this to me", "Print")

### Next Steps
- **Issue:** Generic "Try Another" CTA, no personalized recommendations
- **Impact:** Missed opportunity for engagement
- **For 40-60s:** Need "People who liked this also tried..." suggestions

---

## Accessibility & Age-Friendly Design Issues

### Typography
- **Issue:** Adequate but could be larger, especially on mobile
- **Impact:** Eye strain for aging eyes
- **For 40-60s:** Need generous font sizes (18px+ body text)

### Touch Targets
- **Issue:** Buttons/links are standard size but not oversized
- **Impact:** Harder to tap on mobile for less dexterous users
- **For 40-60s:** Need 44px minimum touch targets (Apple standard)

### Navigation
- **Issue:** No breadcrumbs, no "back to top", no keyboard shortcuts
- **Impact:** Disorientation, especially on mobile
- **For 40-60s:** Need clear wayfinding at all times

---

## Onboarding & Education Issues

### First-Time Experience
- **Issue:** No tutorial, demo video, or "how it works" walkthrough
- **Impact:** Users don't understand the value before trying
- **For 40-60s:** Need explicit education ("Here's how Booey helps you...")

### AI Explanation
- **Issue:** Assumes everyone knows what AI is and trusts it
- **Impact:** Skepticism, hesitation ("What is this AI thing?")
- **For 40-60s:** Need plain-English explanation, "It's like having a helpful assistant"

### Help & Support
- **Issue:** No FAQs, no tooltips, no live chat or contact option
- **Impact:** Users get stuck and leave
- **For 40-60s:** Need abundant help, reassurance, human contact option

---

## Engagement & Delight Issues

### Animations & Micro-interactions
- **Issue:** No transitions, no hover states, no celebration moments
- **Impact:** Feels flat, not delightful
- **For 40-60s:** Need subtle animations (not flashy) to guide attention

### Personalization
- **Issue:** No "Welcome back!", no history preview, no favorites
- **Impact:** Feels transactional, not relationship-building
- **For 40-60s:** Need familiarity, "we remember you"

### Empty States
- **Issue:** No friendly messages when things fail or are loading
- **Impact:** Anxiety during errors
- **For 40-60s:** Need reassuring language, clear recovery paths

---

## Research Topics for Sub-Agents

1. **Visual Design Systems for Mature Audiences** — Illustration styles, color psychology, whitespace usage
2. **Trust Signal Best Practices** — Testimonials, social proof, security badges for non-tech users
3. **Age-Friendly UI Patterns** — Typography, touch targets, navigation for 40-60 demographic
4. **Wizard UX Optimization** — Progress indicators, form design, loading states
5. **Results Page Enhancement** — Content presentation, sharing/saving, personalization
6. **Onboarding for Non-Tech Users** — Tutorials, tooltips, "how it works" best practices
